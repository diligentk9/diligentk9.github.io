About Diligent K9

Previous Next

I want to share my love of dogs and dog training with you! Specifically, I want to do so by teaching you how to manage your dog's behavior, improve your relationship with your dog, and teach your dog to be obedient.

Diligent K9 is founded on the principles of fair and transparent practices, both as a business and with regard to training practices. You will see me employ all of techniques I encourage others to use with my own dog. You will not find me hiding my sources, rather you will find me readily sharing the books, people, podcasts, and other places I obtain information and experience from.

I began training my dog Dean, a German Shepher / Belgian Malinois mix, after adopting him from a local shelter. At almost two years old he didn't even know how to sit. Today, I participate in a variety of activities with my dog, ranging from casual hikes and games of fetch to detailed obedience routines, scentwork, and bitework for protection sports.
